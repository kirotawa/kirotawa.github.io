"Creating a gif from a screencap"

Published: ter 18 agosto 2015
By Kirotawa

In misc.

First you need to recorder you screen. In order to this you can use kazam. After you have recorder you screen.

step one

install imagemagick mplayer

step two

mplayer -ao null -vo jpeg:outdir=path-where-save

step three

convert path-where-save/* image.gif

After this, it's done!
